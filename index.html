ABOUT IDEALAB

Transforming Research Into Actionable Development Solutions

IdeaLab (Lab for Implementation, Development and Evaluation Analytics) is a research & consultancy firm founded by experienced development professionals. We combine rigorous evidence, field insights, and innovative thinking to help organizations design, evaluate, and strengthen impactful development programs.

Our Vision

To become a trusted and leading research and consultancy partner driving evidence-based decision-making for sustainable development.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative and actionable research and consultancy services that empower organizations to design, implement, and evaluate impactful interventions.
